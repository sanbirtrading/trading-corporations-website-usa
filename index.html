About Us

About Us

WE ARE YOUR GATEWAY TO THE WORLD'S MARKETS.

LOUMAGUI is an agricultural commodities trading Marketplace based in the Midwest of USA, specializes in supply chain management of agriculture commodities such as Wheat, Rice, Lentils & Pulses, Beans, Corn, Soybean & Fertilizers. We also specialize in Sugar, Edible Oils, Milk Powders and Frozen Meat. We pride ourselves with an extensive network of supplies from every continent, and our in-house logistics capabilities. This is how we can respond to any demand in the most challenging market. Our focus remains to supply emerging markets, and we are very responsive for emergency request.

We have succeeded in building an excellent reputation as a trusted business partner. Our core values of integrity, fairness and vision arms us to provide excellent services for our clients, and maintain a trust fueled relationship with suppliers and financial institutions.

BUSINESS PRINCIPLE

Our actions are guided by values of ethics, Integrity and 100% stakeholders Satisfaction. We plan with our clients and go the extra step to ensure that the services we provide keep up with the needs of growing businesses.

MISSION

The company was designed to be a supplier of commodities and services. Over the years we’ve become a very reliable procurement company for our customers. Our Focus is on Emerging market and more specifically Sub-Saharan African Markets. We are constantly diversifying our offer to satisfy our clients. We currently service the public sector worldwide, with our focus being US federal government and state agencies. We are also approved to procure to the United Nations Systems. We are CCR and ORCA registered.

VISION

We leverage technology, our relationship with the most reputable distributors and manufacturers to offerthe best value and quality to our customers. Achieving 100% customer satisfaction by delivering quality products and services at an affordable cost. Our forward vision is to strive to become an entity in Comodity Market.
